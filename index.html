Who are we

We are a family ran business in the heart of Manchester. We have been in the coffee and bakery industry for over 40 years, serving a variety of premium coffees from around the world along with our family recipe baked pastries and desserts!

Where to find us

Our state of the art coffee house is located on Oxford Road, perfect for a quick visit whether you're in the morning rush before work or need fast service on your breaks! We also have a seating area if you prefer to relax in a homely quiet area to unwind!
